Bootmachine

Bootmachine is a bootstrapping tool for securely provisioning virtual servers up until the point where customized configuration management begins. The bootmachine's goal is to allow getting started with, maintenance of, and exploring new stack options through a simple, pluggable and highly customizable interface.

Code: https://github.com/rizumu/bootmachine
Docs: https://bootmachine.readthedocs.org/
